Linguists on Mastodon

Follow all your favourite linguists

You want to get in touch with linguists from around the world on Mastodon? This page lists accounts from linguists and linguistic institutions and offers an easy method to follow multiple accounts at once. Just decide if you want to follow all or only some of the accounts listed below. You are provided with a .csv-file, which you can upload in Mastodon in order to follow all accounts included. Here you can find an short instruction, on how to do this.

Accounts by handle, name, and profile link (most recent first)

Full names are only included if they are shown in the profile or on explicit demand. You want to get on the list? Or you don’t want to be on it anymore? Please add yourselves here or contact me via email (preferred) or under @stefanhartmann@fediscience.org.

You are interested in linguistic content on mastodon, but you don’t want to be added to the list? Then you could consider follwing the group @linguistics@a.gup.pe or @linguistics@mastodon.social.
